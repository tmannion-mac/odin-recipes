Description

This is a simple lasagna recipe with very little steps. Making the perfect lasagna doesn't have to be tedious. Follow below to see the ingredients needed and the steps you can follow along!

Ingredients

  • Beef: Ground beef is preffered but ground turkey can be used if you want a lighter option.
  • Spaghetti sauce: You can use store-bought or homemade.
  • Eggs: Eggs help to bind the cheese mixture together.
  • Lasagna sheets: These are an essential.
  • Cheese: You'll need cottage cheese, mozarella and parmesan.
  • Seasonings: Season with dried parsley, salt and black pepper.
  • Water: Pour a half of a cup of water around the edges of the baking dish before baking.

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozarella, eggs, half of the parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna accordingly, using the lasagna sheets to create the layers of beef and cheese.
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.

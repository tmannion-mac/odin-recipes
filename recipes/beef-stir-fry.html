Beef Stir-Fry

Home

Description

This dish takes only 25 minutes to prepare and requires ingredients you probably already have.

Ingredients

  • Diced beef sirlion
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 red bell pepper
  • 2 carrots
  • Minced garlic
  • Soy sauce
  • Chopped onion

Steps

  1. Cook beef on medium-high heat until browned
  2. Add in vegetables and stir-fry for 2 minutes
  3. Add in soy sauce and let simmer
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
